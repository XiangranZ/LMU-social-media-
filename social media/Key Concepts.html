Definitions of Designing and Online Medias

Online media, also referred to as digital media or digital content, describes content that is presented on the internet or through digital channels. Examples of online media are websites, news articles, videos, social medias and more. Key technologies driving platform development are for example 5G Networks, Artificial Intelligence, blockchain and cybersecurity technologies.

Case study: Instagram

User interface and experience design

Instagram is always adujusting to new trends. The app interface includes stories, reels, an explore page, and a personal profile. It has a very personalised algorithm and is beginner-friendly.

Features and functionalities.

Like, comment, Direct message, tagging, posting, live-stream, analytics, verification, polls, close friends story

Target audience and user demographics.

In the past, Instagram is mostly used by the younger generation. However, the user demographics of Instagram is much more diverse now.

Monetization strategies.

  • Influencer marketing collaborations: individuals or brands can engage in sponsored partnerships
  • Advertisement options: feed ads, stories ads, explore ads
  • features like verifications for authenticity

Privacy and security measures.

report, block, no comments, private account, 2-factor-authentification, commnity guidelines

Recent updates and innovations

shared posts, notes, threads, broadcasting channel, paid "premium content"
